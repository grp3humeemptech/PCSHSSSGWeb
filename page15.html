PASIG CITY SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL'S Supreme Student Government

WHAT IS SSG?

The Supreme Student Government is the foremost co-curricular student organization autjorized to operate and implement pertinent programs, project and activities within schools. 

It lays the groundwork for good governance, volunteerism, unity and cooperation by providing the student various venues where they can improve their leadership, knowledge, skills and attitudes.
