INDIGAY 2018
-
PISTANG PINOY


A warm welcome to embrace and remember our roots, culture and tradition. This spectacular event was a successful project of our SSG officers alongside with our school's Filipino department. This was an event that truly awakened each and everyone's nationalistic spirit. A series of competitions and events were conducted such as balagtasans, spoken poetrys, sabayang pagbigkas', masining na pagkwekwento and other tradional games.
